Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Co-Pilot Labs (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and handles your information when you use Tarmac Log (the “App”). We take your privacy seriously and only collect what is necessary for the App to function.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address. This is used to authenticate you and associate your flight data with your account.

Flight Log Data

The App stores flight records you create, including: dates, aircraft, origin and destination airports, flight times (total, PIC, SIC, night, IFR, cross-country), landing counts, crew names, and any notes you enter. This data belongs to you.

Aircraft and Crew Profiles

Aircraft registrations, type information, and crew names you enter are stored as part of your account.

Subscription Status

We store whether your account has an active Pro subscription in order to unlock features. We do not store your payment details — payments are handled entirely by Apple or Google.

Usage Identifiers

A unique user ID is assigned to your account when you register. This is used to keep your data separate from other users’ data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • App functionality: Your email address authenticates you. Your flight data is stored and synced so you can access it across sessions and devices.
  • Subscription management: Your subscription status determines whether you have Pro or Free tier access.
  • Customer support: If you contact us, we may use your email address to respond.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

3. Data Storage and Cloud Sync

Tarmac Log is offline-first: all data is stored locally on your device. When you have an internet connection, your data is synced to secure cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase (our backend provider). Supabase stores data on servers in the United States. You can find Supabase’s privacy information at supabase.com/privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

Tarmac Log uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — authentication and cloud data storage. Acts as our data processor.
  • Qonversion — subscription entitlement management. Receives your user ID and subscription status to verify Pro access. See their privacy policy at qonversion.io/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing for Pro subscriptions. We do not receive your payment details. Apple and Google handle all billing.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties beyond those listed above.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all associated data (flight records, aircraft profiles, crew profiles, and account credentials) is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your flight records are visible in the App at any time.
  • Delete your account and all data — use Settings → Delete Account in the App, or contact us at tarmac-log-support@googlegroups.com.
  • Export your data — export functionality is in development and will be available in a future update.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly from the App (Settings → Delete Account). Deleting your account permanently removes all your flight data, aircraft profiles, crew profiles, and account credentials from our systems within 30 days. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active App Store or Google Play subscription — you must cancel that separately through your device’s subscription settings.

For more details, see our Account Deletion page.

8. Children’s Privacy

Tarmac Log is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at tarmac-log-support@googlegroups.com.
